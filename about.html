About Me

Hello! I'm Ruxin Xie, a Research Associate at Princeton University specializing in architecture, material technology, and parametric design. My passion lies in creating sustainable and innovative designs that push the boundaries of traditional architecture.

With a strong background in digital fabrication and computational design, I strive to integrate cutting-edge technologies with environmentally conscious practices. My work on projects like the Reclaimed Timber Project for BuildFest 2024 exemplifies my commitment to sustainability and material innovation.

I'm currently exploring new directions in mechanical design and material innovation, aiming to contribute significantly to the field of architectural research. Feel free to explore my projects and reach out through the contact page for collaborations or inquiries.
